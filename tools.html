About These Tools

All utilities are built as standalone HTML files with inline CSS and JavaScript. No build tools, no npm packages, no complex setup. Just open and use.

Privacy First: Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly fetch from external URLs or sync to your own cloud storage. No tracking, no analytics, no data collection.
